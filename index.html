About Us

ActiveRank is an innovative recommendation system to make shoopers more aware of how women-friendly the companies they shop at are, and where to shop to better support women employees. In Computer science and mathematics,26% of the workforce are women. In the UK, only 21% of senior roles are held by women. All over the board, there is room for improvement. Consumers have the power to influence companies to be more womne-friendly,and that's where our extension comes in.

Company ratings

Find out how the companies you shop at treat their female employees.

Suggestions

Find out which companies that are more women-friendly are selling what you're buying.

Alerts

Get notifications of companies to avoid. Before you click submit on a shopping site, if the company is especially bad, we'll let you know.

Our Methodology

With our team of statisticians and EEOC experts, we use a hollistic approach to rate companies, using three pillars for rating: salary differences, benefits, and percentage of women in companies. To find out more about our ranking methodology, please contact us at questions@activerank.com.
